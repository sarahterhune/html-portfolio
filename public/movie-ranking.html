The Best Movies According to Sarah

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Beauty and the Beast

This is my favorite Disney movie. I particularly love the scene in the field of dandelions.

Clue

A great take on the classic board game. It brings comedy and suspense while staying true to the nature of my favorite board game.

Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone

The movie that first transported me to this magical world, and allowed me to discover my love of all things Harry Potter.
